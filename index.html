Trunk Or Treat

Located at Marlboro High School our students put together a themed decorated trunk with either, games, snacks, or both for you and your kids to come and experience.

The Marlboro High School Trunk or Treat event, presented by the Peer Leaders, will take place on October 29th from 4:00 to 5:30 PM at the Marlboro High School parking lot. This family-friendly event is designed for children ages 12 and under, with a rain date scheduled for October 30th. Attendees are encouraged to wear Halloween costumes and enjoy over 30 decorated trunks. The event is a nut-free environment, ensuring safety for those with allergies. Snacks and refreshments will be available for purchase, with proceeds benefiting the American Cancer Society. For more information, attendees can contact kmcgeough@frhsd.com.
